# Farmer's Guide to AI Crop Monitor

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up & Onboarding**
   - Visit [App URL] and create an account using your phone number or email.
   - Complete your farm profile: location, size, and primary crops.
   - You're ready to start!

2. **Uploading Images**
   - You can upload images from your phone, webcam, or drone.
   - Navigate to the "Upload" section in the app.
   - Select your image source and follow the instructions.
   - For best results, take clear, close-up pictures of leaves against a simple background.

3. **Understanding Results**
   - After processing, you will see a diagnosis (e.g., "Healthy", "Leaf Blight").
   - Each diagnosis comes with a confidence score and a detailed explanation showing which parts of the leaf the AI used to make its decision.
   - You will receive immediate treatment recommendations (organic and chemical options).

4. **Advanced Features**
   - **Yield Forecast**: Check the "Dashboard" to see predicted yields for the season.
   - **Alerts**: Subscribe to SMS/WhatsApp alerts for disease warnings specific to your region and crop.
   - **History**: View all your previous scans and recommendations in your personal log.

## Best Practices

- Upload images regularly for continuous monitoring.
- Confirm if diagnoses were correct to help the AI learn and improve.
- Combine AI recommendations with advice from local agricultural experts.
